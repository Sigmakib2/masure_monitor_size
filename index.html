How to calculate screen size?

The diagonal length of a monitor is actually the size of that monitor or tv. Measure the diagonal length of the screen. Suppose the diagonal length of a tv screen is 32 inch. So, we can say that tv is a 32 inch tv. But don’t calculate it with the border of the monitor or tv. The border or frame is only for additional support.
